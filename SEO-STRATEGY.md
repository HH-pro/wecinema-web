## 4. Backlink acquisition roadmap

Priority order (effort vs. authority):

**Quick wins (weeks 1–4)**
- Submit to indie-film & startup directories: Crunchbase, Product Hunt, AlternativeTo, SaaSHub, film-platform listicles.
- Filmmaker resource lists: "where to sell short films" articles — request inclusion (you now have a real /marketplace/guide to point to).
- Festival & film-school resource pages: offer WeCinema as a distribution option for student filmmakers.

**HARO / journalist outreach (ongoing)**
- Answer queries on filmmaking, creator economy, streaming, and "side income for creatives." Use Connectively (HARO successor), Qwoted, SourceBottle.
- Pitch angle: founder/expert commentary on indie distribution economics.

**Linkable assets (build once, earn links forever)**
- **Data report**: "State of Indie Film Monetization" using anonymized marketplace stats — journalists link to original data.
- **Free tools**: screenplay format checker, film-budget calculator, royalty/earnings calculator. Tools attract natural links.
- **Free templates**: screenplay template, film release contract, pitch deck — gated only by a link-worthy landing page.

**PR / guest posting**
- Guest posts on filmmaking blogs (No Film School, Filmmaker Freedom, Raindance, Premiumbeat-style outlets).
- Creator-economy newsletters and podcasts (founder interviews).

**Competitor backlinks**
- Run Vimeo OD / FilmHub / Filmsupply / Filmtomarket through Ahrefs/Semrush "Backlink gap" → pitch the same directories, listicles, and resource pages that link to them.

## 5. AI search / Generative Engine Optimization (GEO)

You're already ahead: `robots.ts` welcomes GPTBot, ClaudeBot, PerplexityBot, Google-Extended. To get **cited** in AI Overviews / ChatGPT Search / Perplexity:
- **Answer-first content**: open each guide/FAQ with a direct 2–3 sentence answer (AI engines lift these).
- **FAQ + HowTo schema** on guides (done on `/marketplace/guide`, `/support`, `/welcome`) — extends to comparison pages.
- **Entity clarity**: keep the `Organization` schema (`sameAs` social profiles, `logo`, `contactPoint`) consistent everywhere so engines resolve "WeCinema" as a known entity. Add real social profile URLs to `sameAs` when available.
- **Definitive lists**: "best platforms to sell films" with a table is exactly what AI engines summarize.
